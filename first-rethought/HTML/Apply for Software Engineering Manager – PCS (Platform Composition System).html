Software Engineering Manager – PCS (Platform Composition System)

Beaverton, Oregon

Become a Part of the NIKE, Inc. Team

NIKE, Inc. does more than outfit the world’s best athletes. It is a place to explore potential, obliterate boundaries and push out the edges of what can be. The company looks for people who can grow, think, dream and create. Its culture thrives by embracing diversity and rewarding imagination. The brand seeks achievers, leaders and visionaries. At NIKE, Inc. it’s about each person bringing skills and passion to a challenging and constantly evolving game.

NIKE is a technology company. From our flagship website and five-star mobile apps to developing products, managing big data and providing leading edge engineering and systems support, our teams at NIKE Global Technology exist to revolutionize the future at the confluence of tech and sport. We invest and develop advances in technology and employ the most creative people in the world, and then give them the support to constantly innovate, iterate and serve consumers more directly and personally.  Our teams are innovative, diverse, multidisciplinary and collaborative, taking technology into the future and bringing the world with it.

If you’re ready to innovate and lead in global technology platforms, join Nike’s Global Technology Service Platform organization! You will be part of an organization that is revolutionizing Nike technology platforms and driving a technology landscape that is simplified, modern, flexible and will ultimately enable Nike on its journey to be a platform-oriented company in 2021 and beyond.

Who we are looking for

As a Software Engineering Manager, you will manage a best-in-class distributed software engineering team focused on building and running highly scalable, fault tolerant, and globally distributed platforms that help transform and power Nike's business.

What you will be working on
  • You will help build and deliver an internally developed cloud agnostic PaaS, API Gateway and Service catalog/mesh offerings for the Nike developers ecosystem based on modern technology such as Istio, Kubernetes, and Golang
  • Drive your team to establish standard methodologies, define success and develop critical metrics to continuously evolve feature delivery and scale production operations
  • Own end-to-end Engineering Delivery of your platforms. Support annual operating plans in alignment with roadmap and platform strategy
  • Partner with Technical Product Management to ensure there are requirements and technical direction ready for the team to deliver against
  • Drive/help in breaking down technical work during sprint planning and ensure product and technical features are delivered to spec and on-time
  • Ensure solutions are designed and developed using a scalable and resilient cloud native architecture
  • Coach, mentor, grow, and hire best-in class engineering talent
  • Embrace and embody Nike’s core values (maxims) in your work and interactions with peers, partners, and direct reports
Who you will be working with
  • Partner and engage with platform engineering squads and their leadership to align on platform-centric roadmap & requirements that will support thousands of application and service engineers
  • Work closely with engineering leadership, product leadership, architects, and program to develop and contribute to foundational platform roadmaps
  • Partner with Principal Engineers & Principle Architects to develop tools and frameworks for improving security, reliability, maintainability, availability, scalability, self-serviceability and performance of your platform’s foundation
What you bring
  • Bachelor's degree or higher or equivalent combination of education and experience and training
  • 2+ years of experience in leading technical teams and comes from a technology background (developing teams, building scalable, cloud-based applications)
  • Knowledge of software engineering practices, particularly on distributed systems and cloud native architectures
  • Strong communication, ability to influence, solve complex problems, and leadership ability while being a great teammate are a must
  • Ability to articulate a vision and strategy in a way that inspires and motivates a team and focuses their energy on achieving business goals
  • Ability to bring diverse groups together to solve common goals
  • Focused leader of people and teams dedicated to empowering and growing team members rooted in trust and aligned to Nike's Maxims
  • Pragmatic mindset, highly focused on delivery and customer value rooted in continuous delivery of world class resilient, scalable systems

NIKE, Inc. is a growth company that looks for team members to grow with it. Nike offers a generous total rewards package, casual work environment, a diverse and inclusive culture, and an electric atmosphere for professional development. No matter the location, or the role, every Nike employee shares one galvanizing mission: To bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world.
